About This Excursion

Embark on an extraordinary full-day adventure exploring Nairobi's most celebrated wildlife sanctuaries, conservation centres, and cultural landmarks. This carefully curated journey combines the thrill of an authentic African safari with meaningful conservation encounters and a rich introduction to Kenya's history and heritage.

Your experience begins with a sunrise safari in Nairobi National Park — the only national park in the world located beside a capital city — where you will search for lions, rhinos, giraffes, zebras, buffalo, and a wide variety of birdlife against the dramatic backdrop of Nairobi's skyline. The journey continues through the Sheldrick Elephant Orphanage, the Giraffe Centre, and concludes at the Nairobi National Museum. The perfect balance of wildlife, conservation, and culture — all within one city.

What You'll Experience

Sunrise Safari – Nairobi National Park

Begin your day at first light with a game drive through Nairobi National Park. Search for lions, black rhinos, leopards, buffalos, giraffes, zebras, and over 400 bird species — all with the city skyline as a stunning backdrop.

Elephant Conservation – Sheldrick Orphanage

Watch orphaned baby elephants eagerly run toward their keepers for milk bottles before enjoying their playful mud bath. Hear the individual rescue story of each elephant from their dedicated surrogate keepers.

Giraffe Feeding – Giraffe Centre

Stand face-to-face with endangered Rothschild's giraffes and hand-feed them from an elevated platform. Feel their long purplish tongues as they gently take pellets from your palm — a moment you will never forget.

Kenya's Heritage – Nairobi National Museum

Round off the day at Kenya's foremost museum. Explore exhibitions on natural history, archaeology, art, and the diverse cultures that make Kenya one of Africa's most fascinating countries.
